The Voice

Sometimes this world seems so confusing,
It does feel suffocating..
You always remain in some kind of dilemma,
and feel that may be it is the result of your karma..
But you have to make a choice,
by listening to your heart's voice..
May be it will help in finding your true self,
when you are currently lost in world's shell..
It may be hard at first,
and it may cause you an emotional burst..
But during such "bursts" just hold yourself tight,
and just repeat ,"Everything is gonna be alright"..

Written by Khushboo Gupta, Dec 12, 2022

Oil of my Troubled Soul

You are oil on my Troubled Soul..
Unintentionally though , but keeping me whole...
Can't say much about you..
Yet my whole day revolves around you...
You don't know that but you have changed me..
I have never been like that I am right now , you see...
Deep down there is so much confusion , fear , questions , concerns and dilemmas..
But never ever have I tried so harder to chill and ignore those schemas...
And again The elephant in the room is..
Why am I doing so?
Is it your spell , you or something else?
Right now , I have only one explanation to give...
YOU HAVE BECOME MY HABIT..
A Habit which gives me happiness...
A Habit which is unknowingly teaching me many things..
A Habit which itself showing me how to control this habit...
Over time , I would like to honor it to "Beautiful Titles"..
If only when I would be "Lucky" enough to be entitled...
Sometimes I think , I am fighting with the "Destiny" or "Nature" itself to let me have you..
I will win or lose , only upcoming time will say...
I want to savor our moments..
But I fear...
Would that make it more painful when it all will end..?
Are these all really "Real" ?
Or are they just a trick of my mind as I fear...
I am just so "Addicted"..
Will I lost myself or will get a new "me" projected...
There are just so much "going on" to deal with..
But still I am just avoiding the tough corners and making situations docile enough to play with...
But whatever all this is now..
In future ,whenever I will look "Back to December" somehow...
I will always REMEMBER you ..
As "Oil on my Troubled Soul"...
          - K.G.

The Walk

We were walking ..
walking together..
but i have to stop..
there was some reason..
he got stopped too..
but just for a while..
to turn backwards..
to see if situation is all right..
assumed that he is neglected..
but it was never so..
I needed him to stay...
but still he turn forwards..
began moving..
without turning back any further..
without any wait..
He keep moving..
AND , HE NEVER RETURNS..
